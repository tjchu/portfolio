DIRECTV Internship

June - September 2015

Member of the Product Integration and Testing Team

On the team, I was responsible for creating automation testing scripts for DIRECTV’s broadcasting technologies with software such as CA LISA and JMeter. The automation testing was beneficial to the company because it helps them become more time- and cost-efficient. It also allows the company to adopt the agile method of developing products because they immitate the software environemnt without actually having one.

I was also responsible for testing the NFL Sunday Ticket app by ensuring its features function and flow correctly on various devices such as iOS Tablets/Phones, Android Tablets/Phones, Roku, XBOX, and PlayStations.

Experienced the AT&T/DIRECTV Merger

With the merger of DIRECTV and AT&T, I was responsible for delivering presentations to new executives from AT&T to introduce the benefits of automation testing and agile method.

So trust me when I say I know how to give HIGH LEVEL descriptions to colleagues!

Gained: Excellent Professional Experience

The internship project allowed me to expereince the real work environment for the first time. I deeply understood what the waterfall and agile methods were, how to schedule meetings, best way to communicate with a manager/colleague under each specific circumstance, and the many professional terms used at work.

Moreover, I immensely expanded my professional network throughout the span of three months. Right now, I have a huge wide network of connections in the Los Angeles area and some in the Boston area because many of my colleagues attend(ed) MIT.
